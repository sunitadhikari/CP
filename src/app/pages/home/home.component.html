Aaspatal

Aaspatal is a web-based software that helps in facilitating the functioning of hospitals. It streamlines and integrates the operations, processes and information flow in hospitals and is designed to manage all the aspects of hospitals in a single platform. It combines all the information related to patients, doctors, staff, hospital administration into one software.
